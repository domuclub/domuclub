Crumbcake

Cake

Ingredients Measurements
Butter (or margarine) 1 stick / 8 tablespoons
Sugar 1 cup
Eggs 2 large
Vanilla 1/2 teaspoon
Flour 1 & 3/4 cups
Baking Powder 1/2 teaspoon
Milk 1/2 cup

Mix as listed.
Spread into jelly roll pan.
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.


Crumbs

Ingredients Measurements
Flour 3 cups
Cinnamon 2 teaspoons
Butter (or margarine) 3 sticks / 24 tablespoons
Brown sugar (dark) 1 & 1/2 cups

Mix mixture as listen with hands, forming crumbs.
Lay on top of batter in pan.
Bake 45 minutes.
Enjoy!
